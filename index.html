Welcome to Broken Vessel!

Hi there!
I couldn't be happier to welcome you to the Broken Vessel community and to help you understand what we are doing, what we hope to achieve and how you can be apart of it.

Depression is a silent battle. This is because people under depression never come out in the open to speak about it for various reasons.
Why should we wait for our friends to first commit suicide before we can feel like doing something?

We believe that no matter who we are, a little touch of care makes a whole big difference in our lives and with your help, we hope to extend that to those feeling depressed.

How can you be involved?

Become a Listener

Broken Vessel Listeners are the most important people in this vision. As a listener, you get the opportunity and platform to share "a touch" of care and love to those feeling depressed.

How does it work?
Once you sign up as a listener, you are enrolled in a 20-30 minute online assessment that equips you with the right attitude and skills to be an effective listener, You are then with due time, you are paired with someone whom you will be able to stand with, encourage and help them out of it.
Sounds exciting, right?

Become a Friend

We understand that not everyone can be a listener, but that does not exclude you from playing a part.

  • Doctor
  • Professional Counsellor
  • Social Media Influencer
  • Blogger
  • All those great professions and talents we have not mentioned

We completely rely on you, and here is how exactly?
A listener can only do so much, but we know some of the issues affecting people depressed can not go away just by listening, sometimes actual professionals are needed Reach out to us about how you can be involved and we shall be glad to share some ideas with you
